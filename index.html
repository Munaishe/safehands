ABOUT SAFE HANDS DAY CARE CENTRE
Safe Hands Special Care Centre Is A Centre Of Excellence Promoting Independence And Advocating For Children With Special Needs In An Inclusive Environment registered With The Registrar Of Deeds In Zimbabwe As A Trust.

OUR VISION
To be a renowned centre of excellence in providing quality day care and training to children with special needs in various poor communities of Zimbabwe..

OUR MISSION
To promote early childhood development in the least restrictive environment.
